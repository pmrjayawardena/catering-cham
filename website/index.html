The Canteen

In addition to providing the school community with nutritious and affordable foods,the canteen should be based on good management practices and be finacially self-sustaining..

The school canteen is a small business.Like any business,it requires good manageent practices to be efficient and successful. The purchase of goods is a significant canteen expense and therefore it is critical that this process is organized and efficeient.It is important to buy products at the best price,buy appropriate quantities,ensure safe,high-quality products,order at the appropriate time.

Principal of Aquinas University College
